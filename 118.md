The Ghost You used to have a future. Growing up was a painful tumult at times, but at least you were growing. Now you only have a past - unfinished business to take care of before you can leave this world behind.
Life is precious. You understand that, now that you’ve lost yours.
You just want to help. You just want to be seen. But sometimes even the simplest desires feel so difficult to grasp.
Ghosty ghost, you’re dead.
