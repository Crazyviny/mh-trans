Ch. 1: How The Game Works

Monsterhearts 2 is intended for 3-5 players, and a session takes 2-4 hours. One of the players sitting down at the table takes on the role of the MC, and plays by a different set of rules. You can choose to play this game as a one-shot or across a short campaign of several sessions.
